1. Scope and developer

BioDietix is an Android nutrition-support application developed by Melih Eren. This policy covers the BioDietix mobile application, its configured production backend, Firebase services used by the app, and this legal website. Privacy questions and rights requests can be sent to meliheren2834@gmail.com.

This legal website is static HTML and CSS. It contains no analytics, advertising, tracking scripts, or application-created cookies. GitHub may process normal network and security logs when it hosts the site; those platform operations are governed by GitHub’s own terms and privacy information.

2. Health and medical position

BioDietix handles information that may be sensitive, including blood test values, body measurements, and allergies. BioDietix is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent disease or any medical condition. Its results are supportive nutrition information based on user-provided and available product data.

Users should consult a qualified doctor, laboratory professional, or dietitian for medical advice, interpretation of abnormal results, allergy concerns, symptoms, supplement or medication decisions, and major dietary changes.

4. How information is processed

Uploaded blood test and allergy PDFs

When a user chooses to upload a report, the file is transmitted over an encrypted HTTPS connection to the configured production backend. The backend may extract supported laboratory values or allergy-related text. Application code is designed to use temporary files and remove them after processing, including on failures; BioDietix does not intentionally keep the uploaded PDF as a permanent user document. Derived values and profile indicators may be stored separately on the device and in the user’s Firebase profile.

Firebase Authentication and account storage

BioDietix may use Firebase Authentication for email/password and Google Sign-In. If enabled by app functionality, profile data, derived report results, allergy information, saved product checks, and an optional profile photo may be stored in user-scoped Cloud Firestore or Firebase Storage paths. Firebase and Google may process authentication, device, integrity, and service metadata needed to provide and secure these services.

Local device storage

Some account, profile, allergy, derived lab, product, language, and theme data may be cached locally so the app can preserve state and show previously saved information. Sensitive app data is intended to use an encrypted local Hive store with key material protected by platform secure storage. Clearing app data, using the in-app deletion controls, or uninstalling the app affects local data as described by the device operating system. Signing out alone should not be treated as guaranteed deletion of all cached data.

Open Food Facts product lookup

A product barcode may be sent by the BioDietix backend to Open Food Facts to retrieve product, ingredient, allergen, and nutrition-label information. The product lookup does not need to send the user’s BioDietix account profile or health values to Open Food Facts. Open Food Facts data is community-contributed and may be incomplete, outdated, or inaccurate; users must check the physical package, especially for allergies.

Production backend

PDF analysis and product evaluation may occur on the production backend configured in the installed app. The backend verifies authenticated requests and, in production, is intended to require Firebase App Check. The production operator must separately verify hosting location, subprocessors, security controls, backup behavior, and operational log retention before Play Store submission.

5. Purposes and legal basis

BioDietix processes information only for purposes such as:

  • providing authentication and user-requested app functionality;
  • processing reports and producing supportive nutrition information;
  • saving and synchronizing a user’s profile and product checks;
  • detecting allergy conflicts and explaining incomplete product data;
  • protecting accounts, preventing abuse, and maintaining service security;
  • responding to support, privacy, and deletion requests; and
  • meeting applicable legal obligations.

Depending on the user’s location and the final legal analysis, processing may rely on performance of a user-requested service, explicit consent for health or special-category data, legitimate security interests, or legal obligations. Users are asked for consent before a selected health report is uploaded. The final lawful-basis wording must be approved by counsel.

6. Service providers, disclosure, and sale

Information may be processed by service providers needed to operate the app, including Google/Firebase, the configured backend hosting provider, GitHub for this static website, and Open Food Facts for barcode product lookup. Data may also be disclosed when required by applicable law or necessary to protect users, the service, or legal rights.

BioDietix does not sell personal data or sensitive health data. BioDietix does not use health or allergy data for targeted advertising. Final Google Play “collected” and “shared” declarations must reflect the actual production services, contracts, SDK behavior, and Play definitions.

7. Retention

  • Account and cloud profile information may remain while the account is active and until it is deleted or no longer needed for the stated purpose.
  • Uploaded PDFs are intended for transient processing and are not intentionally retained by application code after the request completes.
  • Local cached information may remain until the user deletes it, clears app storage, or uninstalls the app, subject to operating-system behavior.
  • Limited security, abuse-prevention, legal/compliance, and deletion-request records may be retained only as long as reasonably necessary or legally required.
  • Provider backups and security logs may expire according to verified provider retention cycles and may not be erased immediately from every backup copy.

Concrete production retention periods and transfer locations require final operator and legal confirmation before publication to Google Play.

8. Security

BioDietix uses controls intended to reduce unauthorized access, including HTTPS transport, authenticated user-scoped cloud paths, Firebase App Check in production, encrypted local profile storage, restricted backend secrets, upload limits, and logging designed not to include health payloads. No internet or storage system can be guaranteed completely secure.

Security or privacy concerns can be reported to meliheren2834@gmail.com. Do not email passwords, authentication tokens, or complete medical reports.

9. Your privacy rights

Subject to applicable law, including KVKK or GDPR-style rights where applicable, users may request information about processing, access to their data, correction of inaccurate data, deletion, restriction or objection, portability, or withdrawal of consent. Withdrawal does not affect processing that was lawful before withdrawal and may prevent health-report features from operating.

To exercise a right, email meliheren2834@gmail.com from the address associated with the account and describe the request. BioDietix may request proportionate information to verify account ownership but will not ask for the account password. Users may also have the right to complain to the Turkish Personal Data Protection Authority or another competent supervisory authority. Exact rights, deadlines, controller identity, and complaint routes require final legal review.

10. Account and data deletion

Users can start deletion in the app through Settings → Delete account or use the public account deletion page without reinstalling the app. See that page for the current scope, identity-verification process, local-device limitations, and possible temporary retention.

11. Children

BioDietix is designed for adults and is not validated for pediatric nutrition. It is not intended to knowingly collect children’s health data. Contact meliheren2834@gmail.com if you believe a child’s data has been provided so the matter can be reviewed and handled according to applicable law.

12. Changes and contact

This policy may be updated when BioDietix functionality, providers, or legal requirements change. The effective date at the top will be updated for a new version. Material changes requiring consent will be handled as required by applicable law.
